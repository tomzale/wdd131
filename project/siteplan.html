My Website Planning Document

Site Name

Zale Gamers Hub

This name maintains brand consistency with my YouTube channel while clearly communicating the site's purpose as a central community space for gamers. It's memorable and aligns with gaming industry conventions.

Site Purpose

The Zale Gamers Hub will provide:

  • A centralized platform for gaming tutorial videos
  • Interactive quizzes for popular games and characters
  • Community engagement through comments and live chat
  • Regular updates on gaming news and memes
  • Creator background and brand information

Scenarios

  • Where can I find the latest Killer Bean gameplay tutorials?
  • How can I test my knowledge about Fortnite characters?
  • Is there a community chat for GTA V players?
  • Where are Zale's gaming memes located?
  • How can I submit my own gaming content to the community?
